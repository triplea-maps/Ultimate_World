== Ultimate World: Revised ==

Standard TripleA '4th edition' + 'LHTR' bomber rules apply. However, there are also variant rules to consider.


=== Unit definitions ===
Unit definitions (attack, defend, movement, PU cost)
infantry: 1, 2, 1, 3 (artillery supportable)
artillery: 2, 2, 1, 4 (supports infantry)
armour: 3, 3, 2, 5
halftrack: 2, 2, 3, 6 (supports infantry)
fighter: 3, 4, 5, 11
bomber: 4, 1, 6, 14 (SBR)
submarine: 3, 1, 2, 8 (submerge, sneak attack)
tboat: 2, 2, 2, 7
destroyer: 2, 2, 2, 8 (cancels sub options)
transport: 0, 0, 3, 8 (can carry 3 infantry or 1 infantry and 1 vehicle)
carrier: 1, 1, 2, 14 (3 fighters or 1 fighter and 1 bomber may land on a carrier)
cruiser: 3, 3, 2, 11 (shore bombardment)
battleship: 4, 4, 2, 21 (2 hit, shore bombardment)

bunker: 0, 3, 0, 6 (2 hit, nwo bunker rules, can build from the start of the game)
