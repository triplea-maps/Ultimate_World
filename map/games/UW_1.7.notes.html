== Ultimate World: Glorious Campaign ==
.<
This is World War 2. Germany, under the rule of the Fuhrer, is about to invade Polond which will launch the largest and most deadliest war in history.
Its allies, Japan, Italy and the remainder of the axis or semi axis countries will all serve their purposes. Italy will commence the African Campaign and
seek to control the Mediterranean. The Axis Puppet States will serve as support on all the fronts. Japan, the greatest of Germanies allies, will start on
its march to create a great empire in the Pacific.The Allies will try to stop them on every front, at every battle, in an effort to stop the onslought.
This is the greatest war in history. This is World War 2. This is Ultimate World.

Standard TripleA '4th edition' + 'LHTR' bomber rules apply. However, there are also variant rules to consider.
